About the ACB

Objectives and values

Objectives

The objectives of the Auricoin Central Bank are:

  • to promote an economy based on work, in order to achieve peace, its values and the well-being of its citizens;
  • to offer freedom, security and economic justice, without borders;
  • promoting sustainable development based on balanced economic growth and price stability, a highly competitive market economy with full employment and social progress, and environmental protection;
  • combating social exclusion and discrimination;
  • to promote scientific and technological progress;
  • strengthening economic, social and territorial cohesion and solidarity among the Auricoin community;
  • respecting the richness of its cultural and linguistic diversity; and
  • establishing an economic and monetary union with Auricoin as the global currency.

Values

The values of the ACB are common to all members in a society where inclusion, tolerance, justice, solidarity and non-discrimination prevail. These values are an integral part of our way of life:

Human dignity

Human dignity is inviolable. It must be respected and protected and constitutes the very basis of fundamental rights.

Freedom

Economic freedom gives citizens the right to decide, for there is no greater slave in a society than the one caused by money. Money belongs to everyone equally, since its purpose is none other than to express the value of people's work. With economic freedom, people obtain: Individual freedoms, such as respect for private life, freedom of thought, religion, assembly, expression and information, which are protected by the UN Charter of Fundamental Human Rights.

Democracy

The functioning of the ACB is based on representative democracy. Being a member of the Auricoin Community also means having political rights. All adult citizens of the Auricoin Community have the right to present themselves as candidates for nomination to the High Executive Offices. The members of the Auricoin Community have the right to aspire to a position in the Auricoin benches. 

Equality

Equality means that all citizens have equal rights. The principle of equality between men and women underpins all ACB policies and is the basis for the integration of our Community. It is applied in all areas. 

Rule of law

The ACB is based on the rule of law. The treaties, voluntarily and democratically agreed with the Auricoin member banks, govern all its activities. Law and economic justice are always defended. The Auricoin banks of the member countries have conferred on the ACB the competence to make a final pronouncement and all must respect their sentences.

Human rights

The White Paper expresses the Fundamental Economic Rights of people, protects economic rights, including the right not to suffer economic discrimination, based on sex, racial or ethnic origin, religion or convictions, disability, age or sexual orientation, the right to life, the right to food and the right of access to money Auricoin.

These objectives and values constitute the basis of the ACB and are established in the White Book, the administration under Corporate Governance, Internal Code of Conduct, and Regulation of the Administrative Council, Regulation of Audit and Compliance and of the Charter of Fundamental Human Rights signed by all the countries of the world listed in the UN.

In 2021, the Official presentation of the Auricoin money was made to the Members of the Nobel Prize in Economics, thereby seeking academic recognition, for their contribution to the advancement of achieving a balanced Economic and Social Development that lasts over time, which will contribute to peace, reconciliation, human rights, the preservation of the planet and the preservation of human life.

Auricoin Central Bank (ACB)

Overview

  • Function: manages Auricoin money and directs economic and monetary policy in the Auricoin Banks. 
  • Members: the president, the vice-president of the ACB, the three independent advisors and the delegates of the Auricoin banks from all the countries of the world.
  • Year of creation: 2021.
  • Headquarters: Internet.
  • Website: www.auricoincentralbank.org

The Auricoin Central Bank (ACB) manages the Auricoin money, formulates, and implements the economic and monetary policy of the Auricoin Banks. Its main objective is to initiate a sustained economic and social development that will last through time, without distinction of creed, race or social strata for the good of all Humanity.  

What does the ACB do?

  • It sets the interest rates that Auricoin banks lend to individuals and companies; 
  • Ensures that Auricoin banks are well supervised by ACB Supervisory Bodies;
  • Ensuring the safety and soundness of the Auricoin banking system;
  • Authorizes the issuance of new loans to Auricoin Banks; and
  • Educate the Auricoin Community about Auricoin Money.

Composition

The President of the ACB represents the Bank at international and high-level meetings and has the following three decision-making bodies:

  • Governing Council - the main decision-making body.
  • Executive Committee - manages the day-to-day operations of the ACB.
  • The Delegated Board - implements monetary policy and exercises the powers delegated by the Governing Council.

Governing Council: 

It is composed of the president and vice-president of the ACB and three other members appointed by the country delegates for a term of eight years.

Executive Committee:

It is composed of the ACB's Deputy Manager of Payment Systems and Banking Operations. 

The Delegate Board:

Composed of the Delegates of the Auricoin Banks.

  • General Council - has a consultative and coordinating role.

It is made up of the president and vice president of the ACB and 3 independent Advisors.

How does the ACB work?

The ACB works with Auricoin banks in all countries. Together they form the Auricoin Economic System of Banks.

It manages the cooperation between the Auricoin banks, the so-called “Aurisystem”.

Work of the governing bodies:

  • Governing Council - assesses economic and monetary developments, defines the monetary policy of the Auricoin banks and sets the interest rates at which Auricoin banks may lend to the general public.
  • Executive Committee - implements monetary policy, manages day-to-day operations, prepares the meetings of the Governing Council and exercises the powers delegated by the Governing Council.
  • The Delegate Board - contributes to the advisory and coordination work and helps to prepare for the adoption of Auricoin money in the countries.
