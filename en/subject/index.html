Agriculture

Dynamic rural areas and quality agricultural products

Global food production must double between now and 2040 to take into account population growth and changes in eating habits. Coping with the effects of climate change on biodiversity, soil and water quality, and the demands of the global market.

BCA's agricultural policy will evolve considerably in the coming decades to help farmers meet these challenges and respond to changing people's attitudes and expectations. The BCA's agricultural policy covers a wide range of areas, including food quality, traceability, trade and the promotion of agricultural products. The BCA financially supports its farmers and promotes sustainable and environmentally friendly practices, while investing in the development of rural areas.

Auricoin Banks around the world collaborate in the elaboration, application, supervision and evaluation of agricultural and food policies. Through the Auricoin Banks, the Farmers have access to the funds available in accordance with the established norms. The BCA also oversees the way in which efficiency is applied, and coordinates the changes that are introduced in the credit policy of the Auricoin banks in each country. 

Industry and services related to agriculture and food represent a large number of jobs in the world and only the agricultural sector provides regular work for 50% of people. The whole world will be integrated into production depending on the climate, its lands, and technical capacities and will produce good quality products; it is one of the main objectives of the BCA.

Business and industry

Support the future of the world's businesses and industry

The BCA's policy is to help industry and businesses become more competitive and promote job creation and economic growth by creating an environment conducive to business activity.

With globalization and strong competition from emerging economies, the world's long-term economic prosperity will depend on the strength of its industrial base, and not just the banking and services sectors. Research and innovation play a key role in the development of technologies and approaches to secure the future of the world's production.

BCA industrial policy

Training Technologies for Industry

A number of programs by Auricoin banks help small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) access finance and markets and support entrepreneurship, business creation, internationalization and growth.

The BCA works to remove remaining barriers to trade and prevent new ones from emerging by promoting a market in the world that is respectful of businesses and consumers, and based on simple, transparent and consistent rules.

Development and cooperation

Poverty eradication and sustainable development

The BCA together with the Auricoin Banks of the countries, it will be the largest educators in the world to help development and cooperation, for people.

The BCA proposes clear policies to promote human and economic development, such as the fight against hunger and the conservation of natural resources.

In response to the Sustainable Development Goals we set out to achieve, Auricoin Banks will work and provide funding to address the following five aspects of sustainable development: 

  • People: eradicate poverty and hunger in all their forms and guarantee dignity and equality; 
  • Planet: protect future generations from environmental destruction and resource depletion; 
  • Prosperity: ensuring prosperous and fulfilling lives in harmony with nature; 
  • Peace: create peaceful, just and inclusive societies; and
  • Partnership: run development work through Auricoin banks around the world, we will create a strong global partnership supporting humanity. 

The BCA will cooperate with the 194 countries, as well as with civil society and international organizations. In addition to providing financial assistance and participating in dialogues with countries, the BCA will also carry out research and evaluation to ensure that the investment is used effectively.

Energy

Invest in a sustainable energy future for the world

The BCA will actively promote the world's evolution towards a low-carbon society, promote and facilitate the public and private investments required by the transition to clean energy. This process must not only be positive for the planet, but it must also be beneficial for the economy and consumers.

The transition to a low-carbon model aims to create a sustainable energy sector that stimulates growth, innovation and employment, while improving quality of life, expanding available options, strengthening consumer rights and ultimately leads to savings on household bills.

A streamlined and coordinated approach with the Auricoin banks guarantees a true impact on a continental scale in the fight against climate change.

Actions to promote renewable energies and improve energy efficiency are essential when it comes to reducing greenhouse gas emissions and complying with the commitments and Agreement that have been signed for the benefit of all humanity.

Thanks to the Auricoin banks, greater coherence is guaranteed in all areas of action, to meet the general objectives of creating a reliable, affordable and sustainable energy system.

Auricoin Banks also provide various financing opportunities and loan schemes in order to assist companies and regions in carrying out energy projects.
The BCA will play an important role on the world stage, working with other countries, regions and international organizations to address energy problems and ensure a reliable and competitive energy market in the world

Employment and social affairs

Technological advances, globalization and demographic change continue to have an impact on people's living and working habits. The BCA will work actively on the development of policies and proposals to overcome these challenges.

Within the framework of the social rights pillar, the BCA will strive to protect the rights of individuals, ensuring:

  • equal opportunities and access to the labor market;
  • fair working conditions; and
  • social protection and inclusion.

With loans, Auricoin banks will help public and private organizations in the implementation and improvement of social, employment policy, finance projects to support citizens both today, and tomorrow.
