About this site

The website auricoincentralbank.org is the official website of the Auricoin Central Bank. It is the ideal site for information about Auricoin money and Auricoin banks.

The ACB website offers:

     — the latest news and important events of the Auricoin money. 
     — information about Auricoin banks. 

Who manages the ACB website?

The ACB Communications Department.

In this website, we want to offer links to all this information. We have tried to classify the information in a clear way and describe what the visitor can find through each link.

Visitor satisfaction

The ACB website is designed with you in mind. We collaborate with site visitors to make the structure and language of the site as clear as possible and are constantly looking for ways to improve it.

Visitor testing: We will periodically test the site with visitors to get their feedback on the live site or on new versions or sections, we are working on. We will also use other visitor research techniques, such as field studies, paper prototyping and content categorization.

All the comments and suggestions we receive will be translated by automatic translation into the languages we have installed on this site and then we will analyze them. We will see which the recurring problems are and we will make the corresponding changes, which may consist of adding a link, changing its description or looking for other ways to help the visitor find the information he/she is looking for.

All comments we receive are anonymous and we do not keep any data on the people who use the site or send us their comments.

Visitors

The opinions of our visitors, combined with our analytics tools, help us to understand who is visiting us and what they are looking for.
