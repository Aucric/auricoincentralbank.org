How was the structure of the Auricoin banks planned?

The Auricoin banks are assigned through franchise contracts to people or companies of the same community, it is sought that the people who are in charge of an Auricoin bank in a country, know their laws, culture, language and customs of their people. For this reason it is up to them to inform the community when they start operations.

Auricoin Banks in the World

We propose to have an Auricoin bank in each country, in a maximum period of 7 years, this will depend on the support that the Auricoin money has in the country by the Community, because, if the Auricoin money is not used as a means of payment, no matter how much effort is made, it will be dead.
